# Privacy Policy

## 1. Data Controller

The data controller is **CentriaCyber**.

## 2. Personal Data Collected

We may collect the following data:

* Email address
* User role
* Reservation details (resource name, date and time)
* Login session data

## 3. Purpose of Processing

Your personal data is processed to:

* Authenticate users
* Manage reservations
* Ensure system security

## 4. Legal Basis

Data processing is based on:

* Legitimate interest
* Contractual necessity

## 5. Data Retention

Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes stated above.

## 6. Data Sharing

Your data is not sold or shared with third parties, except when required by law.

## 7. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

* Access your data
* Rectify inaccurate data
* Request deletion of your data
* Restrict or object to processing

Requests can be made through the system administrator.

## 8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data.

# Cookie Policy

## 1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device to ensure proper website functionality.

## 2. Cookies We Use

This website uses only **essential cookies**, such as:

* Session cookies for authentication
* Security-related cookies

No tracking, advertising, or analytics cookies are used.

## 3. Legal Basis

Essential cookies are used based on legitimate interest and do not require explicit consent under GDPR.

## 4. Managing Cookies

You can configure your browser to block cookies. However, doing so may prevent you from using the booking system correctly.

## 5. Changes to This Policy

These policies may be updated. Changes will take effect once published on this page.

## 6. Contact

For any questions regarding these policies, please contact the system administrator.
